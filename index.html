TowTruck

A service for your website that makes it surprisingly easy to collaborate in real-time.

Try it out here

Introducing TowTruck.

Let's make collaborating on the web easier.

By someone you trust.

Built and hosted by Mozilla, Towtruck is open source.

Create together.

TowTruck lets users communicate, co-author, co-browse and guide each other.

Built for the web.

TowTruck is implemented in Javascript; no software or plugins to install, and it's friendly with existing web pages, while still letting developers customize the experience.

How it works

  1. Click the TowTruck button

    When on a TowTruck-enabled site, collaboration is only a click away.

  2. Share the TowTruck link

    Copy and paste the TowTruck link and send to a friend.

  3. Wait for your friend to join

    You'll see a notification when your friend joins your session, and you can then collaborate in real-time!

Features
